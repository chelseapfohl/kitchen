The package contains interior objects for the old village. 
That could be a great contribution to your survival game or you can use it 
together with "Old Village Environment Pack" to create a very specific atmosphere. 

- Two old chiffoniers 
- Oak table 
- Metal bed 
- Two old TV sets 
- Cookstoves (both metal and brick) 
- Kitchen set 
- Two old fridges 
- Rusty sink with broken mirror and shelves 
- Two commodes 
- Couch 
- Small interior props: teapot, metal cups, dripping pan, cutting board, knife and fork 
- Old persian carpets 

All models have normal map textures. Just go to /Prefabs folder and drag  
the model into your scene. FBX files are located into /Models folder.
